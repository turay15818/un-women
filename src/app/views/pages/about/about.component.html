Our Story

UN Women Market Square was born out of a shared vision for a world where women have equal opportunities to thrive in the business world. We recognized the incredible talent, creativity, and resilience of women entrepreneurs, and we wanted to create a space where their voices and products could be heard and celebrated.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower women economically by providing a marketplace where women-owned businesses can showcase their products, connect with customers, and access resources and support to grow their enterprises.
